Education

Saint-Petesburg State University,
Russian Federation

As a Master's student, I worked as a Psychology Lab Assistant on an international project about the Attachment and Biobehavioral Catch-up (ABC) foster-care program. This project was led by Professor Mary Dozier from the University of Delaware, US.

In this role, I helped create and carry out our research, collected data from Russian orphanages, and took care of the lab equipment. I also helped the people participating in our study and made sure they had all the information they needed. To stay up-to-date on the latest research, I regularly read and summarized new studies. Finally, I used my writing skills to help write and revise papers and presentations about our work.

This job helped me understand psychological research better and improved my skills in many areas. It also played a big part in helping me complete my Master's thesis.
